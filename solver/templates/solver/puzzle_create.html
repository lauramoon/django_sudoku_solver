How it Works

On the next page, you can fill in the clues for your sudoku puzzle.

Once you submit your puzzle, you will be told if it is a valid sudoku puzzle.

If it is valid, you may choose to see the solution.

If the clues you enter do not form a valid puzzle, you can choose to see potentially helpful information on why it is invalid.

Caution: the explanations regarding invalid puzzles are still in development; don't expect them to be super useful yet.
